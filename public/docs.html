NurulQuran: Ayat Compare Matrix

Welcome to the advanced Quranic analysis matrix. This tool is designed to provide seamless comparative insights into Quranic text, complete with offline capabilities, visual syntax highlighting, and audio syncing.

Core Features

  • Side-by-Side Comparison: Compare unlimited Ayahs horizontally or vertically.
  • Intelligent Highlighting: Differences between the master reference (the first card) and comparison cards are highlighted automatically.
  • Offline Matrix: After pressing Init Matrix, the entire Quran is stored locally in your browser so you can search and compare offline instantaneously.
  • Audio Sync: Tap the play button to hear audio. If multiple cards are open, the system will play them continuously down the list.

Search & Match Workflows

  • Global Arabic Search: Type any Arabic phrase into the search bar and press Enter. The tool will automatically find all matches, pick the top 9, calculate the shortest one, and place it at the top as the master reference!
  • Exact Ayah Search: Type 1:1 or 114:6 into the search bar to jump instantly to an exact Surah and Ayah.
  • History Memory: Tap the search bar to see your recent search terms. Tap the x to remove a specific one.

UI Actions & Buttons

  • Sync: Synchronizes the text for all empty feeds based on their current Surah and Ayah number.
  • + Add Feed: Adds a new, empty feed card to your workspace.
  • Undo: Restores the last feed card you closed by accident.
  • Log Data: Saves your current active comparisons into the Comparison Logs history. You can tap on a log to instantly reload those exact cards.
  • Export Vis: Takes a beautiful screenshot of your current screen for sharing.
  • Export HTML: Downloads a standalone HTML file containing your active Ayats along with exact word counts, letter counts, and diff statistics.
  • Init Matrix: Downloads the complete offline database of the Quran to your browser. Required for instant offline searching and text synchronization.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Alt + N : Add an empty feed card.
  • Ctrl + S : Log current comparison data.
  • Ctrl + Z : Undo closing a card.
  • / : Focus the global search bar.
  • ? : Show shortcut toast reminder.
